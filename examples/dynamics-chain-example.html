About Dynamics Processors

  • Compressor: Reduces dynamic range by attenuating signals above the threshold. Use parallel compression (mix control) for gentle control.
  • Gate: Attenuates signals below threshold to remove noise. Hold time prevents rapid opening/closing.
  • Limiter: Brick-wall limiting prevents signal from exceeding the ceiling. Lookahead provides clean limiting without distortion.
  • Glue Compressor: Vintage-style bus compressor with stepped controls for "gluing" mix elements together.
