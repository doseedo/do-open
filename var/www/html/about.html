About


At Doseedo, we're on a mission to revolutionize the way sound is experienced in the digital realm, offering fast, accessible sound effects to everyone without the hassle of browsing through extensive sound libraries. Our vision is a world where AI and human creativity harmoniously coexist, enhancing each other's capabilities.

Doseedo is not just about replacing traditional sound design methods; it's about augmenting the creative process. Our AI is designed to create alongside people, not to take over. We believe in empowering artists, filmmakers, game developers, and content creators with the tools they need to bring their visions to life with ease and efficiency. By analyzing video content, our platform generates contextually appropriate sound effects swiftly, making sound design accessible to all, regardless of experience or resources.

We're committed to providing a cost-effective, user-friendly alternative to traditional sound design methods. At Doseedo, we're more than just a service; we're a community of creative minds dedicated to innovating sound design. We value your feedback and invite you to share your thoughts and experiences with us, helping us evolve and better cater to your creative needs. Join us in redefining sound design, where AI meets creativity, at Doseedo.
