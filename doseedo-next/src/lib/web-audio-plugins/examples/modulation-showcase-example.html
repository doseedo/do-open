About Modulation Effects

  • Chorus: Creates rich, ensemble-like sounds by layering multiple delayed and detuned copies
  • Flanger: Produces the classic "jet plane" whoosh effect using short delays with feedback
  • Phaser: Creates sweeping notches using cascaded all-pass filters
  • Tremolo: Modulates amplitude for rhythmic volume changes; Auto Pan sweeps stereo position
